The Algorithm

Build Cost Calculation

Initial Build Cost = Hourly Rate × Build Time

Monthly Maintenance Cost = Hourly Rate × Maintenance Hours per Month

Total Build Cost = Initial Build Cost + (Monthly Maintenance Cost × Time Horizon)

Subscription Cost Calculation

Total Subscription Cost = Monthly Subscription Cost × Time Horizon

Comparison & Recommendation

Savings = Total Build Cost − Total Subscription Cost

If Savings > 0: Recommend Subscribe

If Savings < 0: Recommend Build

Break-Even Analysis

Break-Even Point = Initial Build Cost ÷ (Monthly Subscription Cost − Monthly Maintenance Cost)

This tells you at what month subscribing becomes cheaper than building.

Key Assumptions

  • Your time has real monetary value
  • Maintenance includes bug fixes, security updates, feature improvements, and infrastructure management
  • Subscription costs and maintenance hours remain constant over the time horizon
  • The comparison doesn't factor in opportunity cost, learning curve, or reliability differences
